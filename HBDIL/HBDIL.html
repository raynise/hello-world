Happy Birthday Irna

Irna last year you said you wanted to walk into 40 "with purpose and delight" when you invited a few close friends to write to you. Tis year, for your 40th birthday, I invited a few friends to submit reflections, wishes, thoughts, photos, or other digital mementos that represent purpose and delight.

Below is a small collection from us. We love you and wanted to present you with a small virtual birthday card as a way to say Happy Birthday!

Raynise

Every year when your birthday rolls around, I always wonder if I have told you the same things before. Then I realize, even if I have, it's okay. Because you continue to be full of integrity and love every turn around the sun. Every year of friendship, I remain in awe of how great one human can truly be. It is truly an honor to have you in my life. I am so grateful to have such a beacon of light one text away.

I often talk about your love of anatomical hearts. I have a deep obsession as well with the heart as an organ, mostly because of the anatomy class I took my senior year, a few months before we met. Did you know that they have created a 3D printed heart? It fails to beat for more than 30 minutes. After so many beats the material falls apart. Scientists are trying to determine the reasoning. I have a theory about the material not being durable for the electricity of the heart. I also have these theory that your love of anatomical hearts stems from all the heart requires to beat. Despite being it's own organ and the center of the circulatory, the heart requires so many other small parts to beat properly. And I am so happy that we can be small parts to each others' hearts.

I hope 40 is everything you hoped for my nigga.

Nicci
